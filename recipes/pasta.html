Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Pasta sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps to Prepare :

  1. In a cooking pot, bring lots of water with salt to boil (4-5 cups) firstly, for cooking pasta. If you taste the water, it should be salty like sea water. That's the common reference.
  2. Then once the vegetables are half cooked, in goes all the spice powders. Turmeric, black pepper, chilli powder, garam masala powder.
  3. Finally, garnish with coriander leaves. Simple and easy Masala pasta is ready to serve!
